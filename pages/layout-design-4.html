College of Science, Engineering, and Mathematics

Welcome

The College of Science, Engineering and Mathematics (CSEM) offers majors which provide training to undergraduate students in the natural sciences, computer science/engineering and mathematics, which facilitate entrance into careers in private industry, government, or graduate and professional schools.

Objectives

To provide specialized training for students in the Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) fields. To produce graduates who are prepared for entrance into graduate and professional schools or STEM-related careers.
